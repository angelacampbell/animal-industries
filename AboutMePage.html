About Me

I am a sixteen year old girl and around three years ago I made the decision to cut meat out of my life completely Recently I have gone vegan because I found out the truth behind the dairy industry and no longer wanted to contribute to it. The Truth About the Milk Industry. On top of avoiding food products with dairy or meat, I avoid clothing products with fur or leather, I purchase vegan friendly soaps, shampoos, makeup products, etc. I also make sure that these products are not tested on animals. Additionally, I avoid circuses with animals because I do not have the heart to pay these people to treat the animals terribly.
